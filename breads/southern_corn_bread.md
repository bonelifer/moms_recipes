# Southern Corn Bread
<HR>

## Ingredients
```
1 c. corn meal
1 well-beaten egg
1/2 t. soda
1 c. buttermilk
1/2 t. salt
1 T. melted shortening
```

## Directions
1. Sift dry ingredients well, add egg & buttermilk throughly mixed.
2. Add shortening & pour into greased shallow pan
3. Bake at 450Â°F for about 18 min.
